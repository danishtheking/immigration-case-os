## Common Forms Reference

### Employment-Based Petitions

| Form | Purpose | Fee (2025) | Processing |
|------|---------|------------|------------|
| **I-140** | Immigrant Petition for Alien Workers | $700 | 6-12 months (standard), 15 days (premium) |
| **I-129** | Petition for Nonimmigrant Worker (H-1B, L-1, O-1) | $460 | Varies by category |
| **I-907** | Request for Premium Processing | $2,805 | Guarantees 15-day adjudication |
| **ETA-9089** | PERM Labor Certification | $0 | 6-18 months |

## Form Packages by Visa Type

### O-1A Petition Package
1. ✅ Form I-129 (Petition for Nonimmigrant Worker)
2. ✅ O-1 Classification Supplement
3. ✅ Form G-28 (Attorney appearance)
4. ✅ Form I-907 (if requesting premium processing)
5. ✅ Petition support letter
6. ✅ Evidence of extraordinary ability (organized by criterion)
7. ✅ Itinerary or summary of events/employment
8. ✅ Consultation letter from peer group or labor organization
9. ✅ Beneficiary's CV/resume
10. ✅ Copy of passport and current visa/I-94

### EB-1A Petition Package
1. ✅ Form I-140 (Immigrant Petition for Alien Workers)
2. ✅ Form G-28
3. ✅ Form I-907 (optional premium processing)
4. ✅ Petition support letter
5. ✅ Evidence of extraordinary ability (3+ criteria)
6. ✅ Beneficiary's CV/resume
7. ✅ Passport copy
8. ✅ Evidence of intent to work in area of expertise

### EB-2 NIW Package

5. ✅ Evidence of advanced degree or exceptional ability
6. ✅ Evidence of proposed endeavor
7. ✅ Letters of support / recommendation
8. ✅ CV/resume and publications list

### I-485 Adjustment of Status Package

4. ✅ Form I-693 (sealed medical exam)

## Filing Tips

- **Always check USCIS.gov for the most current fees** — fees change periodically
- **Use the correct edition** of each form — expired editions are rejected
- **Sign in blue ink** for paper filings
- **Keep copies** of everything submitted
- **Send by tracked mail** (USPS Priority, FedEx, UPS)
- **Check the correct filing address** — varies by form and jurisdiction
- **Premium processing** is available for I-140, I-129, and select others

## Important Notes

- Filing fees and form editions change frequently — always verify on USCIS.gov
- This guide is for reference only — an attorney must verify all filing requirements
- Some forms require biometrics appointments (additional $85 fee may apply)
- Filing addresses vary by state and form type — check USCIS filing instructions
